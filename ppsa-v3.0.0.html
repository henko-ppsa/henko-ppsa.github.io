About Us

Why we exist. For years, we watched world-class European processing and packaging technology struggle to gain ground in Africa. The challenge was never the equipment. It was the missing local partner. PPSA was founded to close that gap with regional insight, hands-on support and a focus on long-term results.

What we do. We are a family-owned company representing leading manufacturers such as Pattyn, Gerstenberg Services, API Schmidt-Bretten and Daniatech. Our work spans Sub-Saharan Africa, where clients rely on us for solutions that fit their exact needs.

How we work. Our strength lies in how we operate. We learn your process, build partnerships that last, and stay involved long after installation. Clients trust us because we support them when it matters most.
