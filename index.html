About Us

Built on partnership, expertise, and a commitment to your success

Why We Exist

For years, world-class European processing and packaging technology struggled to gain ground in Africa. The challenge was never the equipment—it was the missing local partner. PPSA was founded to close that gap with regional insight, hands-on support, and a focus on long-term results.

What We Do

We are a family-owned company representing leading manufacturers such as Pattyn, Gerstenberg Services, API Schmidt-Bretten, and Daniatech. Our work spans Sub-Saharan Africa, where clients rely on us for solutions that fit their exact needs.

How We Work

We grow when you grow. Our approach is rooted in understanding your process, building partnerships that last, and staying involved long after installation. Success is shared—because when our clients thrive, so do we. That's how strong relationships are built.
