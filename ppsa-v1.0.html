About Us

Excellence in Process. Precision in Packaging.

PPSA is a family business focused on bringing reliable processing and packaging solutions to companies across Sub-Saharan Africa. We’ve built our name around a simple idea: every client deserves the right solution for their specific need, backed by people who genuinely care.

We represent a portfolio of industry leaders including Pattyn, Gerstenberg Services, API Schmidt-Bretten and Daniatech. Each partner brings world-class expertise, and we bring the regional insight and hands-on support that help those solutions succeed in Africa.

Great technology is only part of the story. What sets us apart is the way we work. We take the time to understand each operation, build long-term relationships and stay involved long after installation. Our clients know they can count on us for practical advice, quick support and a team that shows up when it matters. This combination of trusted partners, experience and real engagement is what drives PPSA, and it’s why we’re proud to support the industries that keep Africa moving forward.
