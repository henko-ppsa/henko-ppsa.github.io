About Us

For years we saw great European packaging and processing technology struggle to reach Africa, not because of the technology, but because of not having the right partner who understands the local needs. PPSA was created to bridge that gap with local market understanding, required technology insight, hands-on support and results that matter.

As a family-owned business, we provide reliable processing and packaging solutions across Sub-Saharan Africa. We proudly represent world-class partners such as Pattyn, Gerstenberg Services, API Schmidt-Bretten, and Daniatech.

What makes us different is our approach. We take the time to understand each operation, each need, build long-term relationships, and stay involved well beyond installation. Our clients count on us for practical guidance, fast support, and a team that delivers when it matters most.
